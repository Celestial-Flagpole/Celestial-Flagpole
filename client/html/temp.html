Instructions

You can play a video in Floatie in 2 ways:

  • Select the url of the video in your address and drag the selection onto the Floatie screen. The video will begin playing immediately.

  • Search a term in the search bar on the right hand side of the Floatie screen. Then select the one you would like to play.

    • NOTE: Floatie currently only plays youtube videos. Our team plans to add functionality to view videos from multiple platforms. Keep tight!
